Facebook

Facebook is an American social media and social networking service owned by the American technology conglomerate Meta. Created in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg with four other Harvard College students and roommates, Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes, its name derives from the face book directories often given to American university students. Membership was initially limited to Harvard students, gradually expanding to other North American universities.

Instagram

Instagram, a popular photo and video-sharing social networking service, is owned by Meta Platforms, formerly known as Facebook, Inc. It was founded in 2010 by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger. Instagram was acquired by Facebook in April 2012. The company is headquartered in Menlo Park, California.

LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a social networking platform primarily used for professional networking and career development. It allows users to connect with colleagues, potential employers, and other professionals, share their professional experiences, and search for job opportunities. It also facilitates the organization of offline events, group creation, and the sharing of articles, photos, and videos.
